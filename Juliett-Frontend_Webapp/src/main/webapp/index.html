Brutus Course Planner

Are your still struggling with arranging your courses for next quarter?
Feel like stabbing CAESAR? No need, we'll help you get a good schedule without
having to hunt around all the department lists.

Here, you just need to enter some basic preferences and information, tell us what
you want to take, and we'll find the best course schedules for you!

Get Started

Here's how it works:

Who are you?

First we take some basic information about your lifestyle. Nothing too intrusive, just your scheduling preferences
and how far you are from your classes.


What are you taking?

Then, we get your course preferences.We only need the names of the courses! We'll show you which time to take the class based
on the other classes you take and when you want classes to start/end.


A gift for you!

Finally, we work our magic and send back to you a few schedules that work based on your preferences!
From there, all you have to do is put those classes/times into your shopping cart on CAESAR and hit register.




We're not a substitute for CAESAR, but we do make that hellhole a bit less painful. Good luck and happy course-hunting!
